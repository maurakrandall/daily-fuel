v3 · What changed 02 / 12
Onboarding · 1a
New

A handshake,
not a legal form.

Why this matters

Bootcamp feedback: users hesitate before sharing medication-adjacent personal data. We slow down, make three protective commitments plain, and use the warmest version of the sun — small, settled. The handshake before the data.

Header
"Your data stays yours." — italicized "yours" in Fraunces.
Body
No sale. No insurer sharing. No advertising profile. Three plain promises.
Sun
Small (88px), settled (vitality 0.45). Not performative — a quiet pilot light.
Link
"Read our full privacy policy" — styled, non-functional (engineering scope).

New

Targets are infrastructure,
not a progress bar.

The principle we're protecting

Targets power the gap math behind the scenes. But the user just sees a horizon that warms — no target line, no endpoint. Adding a marker would build a progress bar wearing a sun costume. We don't do that.

Pill presets: 60g, 80g, 100g, 120g protein · 25g, 30g, 35g fiber. + custom field. Same language as Go-Tos.

A2 · How it threads the system

The horizon starts here.

Feeling good
"Any go-tos so far today?"
Sub: Even something small counts toward today.
Not hungry
"Even a shake or coffee counts. Anything so far?"
Sub: Sometimes autopilot does more than we think.
Rough day
"Anything today? Even the small stuff counts."
Sub: We'll build from wherever today started.
Behavior · A2 taps

Tapping a card means "I already ate this." It does not appear in the plan that follows. The grams credit toward the running total. The horizon warms. A gentle "from earlier" chip carries the foods into Screen B as quiet context.

Behavior · Skip path

"Skip — start fresh" takes the user straight to Screen B with totals at 0 and the horizon visible. Empty isn't failure — it's a fresh start. The gap bar is always present from B onward.

Visual continuity

Atmosphere from the state selector carries through. Feeling good → golden tone. Not hungry → overcast. Rough day → dawn. The running total sits inline next to the horizon — they are one visual system, introduced together.

Updated

Search as discovery,
not data entry.

Why inline pill style

A floating sticky or full-screen search overlay would feel like a different app. The bar matches the language of the food pills — same warmth, same family. Searching is a discovery moment, not a database query.

Placeholder
"Search for something that sounds good"
On add
Confirmation: "Added to today's plan — and saved to My Foods." Warm chip animates in.
Empty state
"Nothing close yet. Try another search."
My Foods
Same pattern, different copy. "Search for foods you already love" → "That's in your rotation now."

Copy + structure
Updated

Warmer category names. Targets at the top of My Goals.

Will eat · Won't eat · My Foods
Protein-primary → Protein Boosters
Protein + fiber (Doubles) → Double Wins
Nuts & Seeds → Crunchy Staples
Vegetables & Fruit → Fresh Add-Ons
Support foods → Gentle Helpers
Why rename

Functional names → human names. "Support foods" feels clinical. "Gentle Helpers" feels like a friend. Bootcamp users responded warmly to the latter language.
