Phase 06
Design notes
The principles that shaped the system, the moments worth obsessing over, and what's open for the next round.
06 / 06

Principles

  • Time of day as the metaphor. The app is called Daily Fuel — the sun rises with the day, with your totals, with your mood.
  • Horizon, not progress bar. No percentage, no target. The sun simply rises as you build.
  • State-responsive atmosphere. Cream stays. Warmth shifts: golden hour, soft midmorning, pre-dawn quiet.
  • Celebration without confetti. A bloomed sun, italic gold serif macros, real-win language.
  • Honest copy. Headline driven by totals. Secondary message driven by state. Both-zero gets a tail, not silence.

Hero moments

  • Vitality-driven sun. One value 0–1 drives core, rays, glow, pulse. Rough days hit "warm" sooner, so 3 items feel like a win.
  • Food card bloom. Tap → gold halo, italic Fraunces macros fade in: "~9g protein + ~4g fiber!"
  • Celebration bloom. Sun scales from 0.55 → 1, near-full radiance, settled.
  • End of day. Sunset picker with three warmly differentiated cards. Closing screen has no button — you simply close the app.

Next round

  • Motion language. Codify card-bloom, sun-rise lerp, screen-transition with a motion library (popmotion / framer-motion).
  • Notification arc. Wire 5 messages to shot-cycle position; reflect tone in the in-app sun on arrival.
  • Saved plan, completed. Refine the "settled, not stuck" feel — maybe a quieter color overlay.
  • Empty plan accessibility. Increase tap targets on minimal-session "Add foods" entry.
  • Real photography or illustration? Decision point for Phase 2 — sun system is identity-strong on its own.
