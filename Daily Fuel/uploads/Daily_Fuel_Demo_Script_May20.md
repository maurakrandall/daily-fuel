### [SLIDE 2 — WHY IT MATTERS] ⏱ ~20 sec

> "Thirty million Americans are on GLP-1 medications. Their relationship with food has fundamentally changed — not temporarily, permanently. 88% aren't hitting protein. Zero — *zero* — in the most cited study met fiber recommendations. And the tools they've always used — MyFitnessPal, calorie counters, meal plans — were built for a different body. Nobody's built for this one."

*Advance.*

---

### [SLIDE 3 — WHAT IT IS / WHAT IT'S NOT] ⏱ ~20 sec

> "Daily Fuel is a nutrition companion for people on GLP-1s. It's a gap-closer, not a tracker. You tell it how eating feels today — good day, rough day, not hungry — and it builds a plan from foods you already eat. No logging. No calories. No streaks. The user sees what they're *gaining*, never what they're missing."

*Advance.*

---

### [SLIDE 4 — VISUAL DIRECTION: GOLDEN HOUR] ⏱ ~15 sec

> "The design direction is called Golden Hour. Warm, not clinical. The sun rises as your totals grow — no progress bar, no target line. Just a horizon that warms. You're looking at what the designed version will feel like."

*Let them look at the mockups for a beat. Advance.*

---

### [SLIDE 5 — V1: IN AND OUT] ⏱ ~15 sec

> "V1 is the smallest thing that proves the thesis. You can see what's in — onboarding, state-based plans, the celebratory selection model, Close the Gap, shot-cycle notifications. What's deliberately out: calorie data — forever. Macro tracking — that's earned later. Community — the long game. This version answers one question: does meeting someone at their lowest motivation moment actually work?"

*Advance.*

### [LIVE DEMO ON EMBER] ⏱ ~90 sec

**Demo flow — practice this path exactly:**

1. **State selector** (~10 sec)
   - "This is what you see every morning. Three states. No wrong answer."
   - *Tap "Feeling good."*

2. **Daily plan loads** (~15 sec)
   - "It pulled foods from my profile — things I said I eat during onboarding. Protein + fiber tagged on every card. Estimated totals running up top."

3. **Tap 2–3 food cards** (~20 sec)
   - *Tap Edamame* → "See that? Micro-celebration. Shows me what I just added — about 9 grams of protein and 4 of fiber. Bar fills. Total updates."
   - *Tap Protein shake* → "Another tap, another win. No checkboxes. No logging."

4. **Save my plan** (~15 sec)
   - *Tap "Save my plan"*
   - "Celebration screen — it echoes what I lined up. State-aware copy. Tomorrow hook built in."

5. **Saved plan view** (~15 sec)
   - "This is my home screen for the rest of the day. Plan lives here until midnight. I can come back, reference it, or close the gap if I want more."

6. **Close the Gap** (~15 sec)
   - *Tap "Close the gap"*
   - "Round two. These are universal suggestions — not limited to my profile. If I find something new, I can add it to My Foods permanently. Discovery built into daily use."

*Switch back to slides.*

## FEEDBACK PROMPTS TO LISTEN FOR

If the group is quiet, these are good questions to seed:

- "Does the state-based model make sense to you? Would you actually pick a state every morning?"
- "What's your gut reaction to no calorie data at all?"
- "Does Close the Gap feel like a natural second step or is it confusing?"
